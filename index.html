FAQ

What is Ponyforge?[+]

A website with six queer ponies. You drag them around, put hats on them, and press buttons to see what happens. That's about it.

Who are these ponies?[+]

Iris, Vesper, Onyx, Prism, Sable, and Femme Fatale. They each have their own pronouns and their own look. They didn't ask to be here, but they seem fine with it.

Why are they slightly wrong?[+]

Because perfect is boring, and also because this was made by one person on a computer. The wrongness is on purpose. Mostly.

Can I keep a pony?[+]

You can't take them off the site, but they'll be here when you come back. They're not going anywhere.

Is this art or a toy?[+]

It's a website where you put hats on ponies. Whether that's art or a toy is not really my department. I just made it.

Who made this?[+]

Oliver Newth made this. That's about all there is to say about it.
