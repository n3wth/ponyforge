FAQ

What is PONYFORGE?[+]

A small chaotic pony maker. You drag, dress, and unleash. It is a website, a toy, a feeling, and a low-grade haunting. We refuse to pick one.

Who are these ponies?[+]

Six full-time ponies and however many you spawn. Each one has pronouns, a snack of choice, and at least one unresolved feeling. Long-press to read their lore.

Why are they slightly wrong?[+]

Because correctness is for tax software. The wrongness is the affection. We let the eyes catch a little too much light and call it a feature.

Can I keep a pony?[+]

Open a lore card and hit Share. You'll get a deep link to that exact pony. Bookmark it. Send it to a friend. The pasture will remember.

Is this art or a toy?[+]

Yes. It is also a love letter, a rehearsal, and a mild prank. Print the field guide if you need something to hold.

Who made this?[+]

A small herd of queer humans and one very patient computer. Built in vanilla HTML, CSS, and JS — no framework, no funnel, no growth team.
